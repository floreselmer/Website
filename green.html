Green

Green is a warm color. Green is appealing because it's a color of balance and harmony. Its smart to use this color because it's a balance of both logical and emotional. Companies that use green are Android, Animal Planet, Land Rover, and Lacoste. Green brings along many feelings, for example green stimulates restfulness, cheerful, soothing, and health-giving. Green represents the outdoor environment such as nature and or organic quality. Green is said to alleviate depression, anxiety, and nervousness because it reinforces hope, health, and adventure. Green in Western culture represents money and being financially stable. In Ireland green is good luck, in Mexica green stand for independence, in Middle Eastern countries green represents fertility. In China green hats are taboo because it signifies that a man's wife has committed adultery.
