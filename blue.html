Blue

Blue is a cool color. Blue is a color that is aesthetically appealing because the color represents calmness and restfulness. Blue is the preferred color of men and is the most used color for brands to promote their products. Blue promotes trust, reliability, and loyalty which is why brands like to use the color to consumers. An example of this is the companies Continental, AirTran, and JetBlue. They use blue to help fight the negative feelings people have with air travel. Blue brings along peace, trust, reliability, loyalty, wisdom. Some studies show that having a room painted blue can make a person more productive and can enhance the surrounding area with energy. Blue has many meanings around the world. In middle eastern countries blue represents the symbolic meaning of heaven or immortality. In china blue is a feminine color unlike in many western cultures.
