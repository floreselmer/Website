Red

Red is a warm color. Red is a color that is appealing because it creates a sense of urgency. This includes sales, appetite, excitement, and passion. Companies that use this color are Mc Donald's, Target, and Coca Cola. Red is also used to express love such as in Valentine's day. Red is a bright color that invokes strong emotions such as love, warmth, comfort, excitement, and even anger. Red has many meanings around the world. In Indian culture, red signifies fear, fire, wealth, power, love, beauty, and purity and is important for marriage. In south African countries it's associated with mourning and or worn in funerals. In Chinese culture red is worn in weddings unlike in western cultures where white is the traditional color.
