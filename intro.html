"About Me"

My education is very important to me because my parents have sacrificed a lot so I can get an education.
The reason why I chose to take this course is because I am required to take it, but I also love working with computers. Ever since I was a kid, I remember spending my days playing around with my windows 95 computer, it's some of the best memories I have. Whether it was just playing games or trying to connect to the internet,I always enjoyed the adventures that I had with my personal computer.

I came to the United States when I was 5 years old, and I remember being so scared to move into a new country that I couldn't eat for days.
You don't really know what to expect when you're a kid, but I have learned so much throughout the years. I am grateful each day for the opportunities that I have, and I try my hardest to learn to the best of my ability in order to become a better person. Something that resonates with me is what the great coach Jim Valvano said in a speech Every Single Day, in Every Walk of Life, Ordinary People do Extraordinary Things. I may be an ordinary person, but with hard work I can do extraordinary things.

6 XHTML Rules

  • Elements must nest symmetrically.
  • Element names are case sensitive. (Convention is to use lowercase values.)
  • End tags are required.
  • Empty elements are signified with a closing slash.
  • Attribute values must be contained in quotation marks.
  • Attribute-value pairs must be written in full(e.g. checked:checked;).

HTML related terms

Attribute:
Code added to an element to provide more information about the element.
Browser:
Application software that allos users to access and view html documents and web pages.
Search Engine:
A program that finds web pages, sites, images, or information based on inputted criteria.
Document Type Definition:
A set of rules that contain all the elements, attributes, and usage rules for the markup language you are using.
Meta Data:
Information about the document itself,such as to present the document,or what other documents are related to the current one,such as style sheets.
