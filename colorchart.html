After doing the research for each color I still liked blue more than the other colors. The main reason I like blue is because I feel like most people can relate to the emotions that its portraits. Just by looking at blue we as humans feel a sense of calmness, trust, and reliability. These feelings are important to my theme. I was mostly fascinated by the fact that blue is not only considered to be masculine but also feminine, especially in China.
